Hexagon Generator

Creates hexagonal patterns (honeycomb) on faces or sketch regions. Useful for weight reduction, ventilation, decorative patterns, and structural lightweighting in 3D printed parts.

Quick Start

  1. Click Solid → 3D Print Tools → Hexagon Generator
  2. Select the face to pattern (or a sketch region)
  3. Set hexagon size and wall thickness
  4. Choose Cut (through-holes) or Emboss (raised pattern)
  5. Click OK

Parameters

  • Hexagon Size — flat-to-flat distance of each hex
  • Wall Thickness — material between hexagons
  • Include Boundary — whether partial hexes at edges are kept
  • Depth — how deep to cut or emboss

FDM Tips

  • Wall thickness ≥ 2 × nozzle diameter (0.8mm for 0.4mm nozzle)
  • Larger hexes print faster with less support
  • For structural lightweighting: 10–15mm hex, 1.5mm walls
  • For ventilation: 5–8mm hex, 1mm walls
